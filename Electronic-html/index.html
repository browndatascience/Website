DataFest by American Statistical Association

ASA DataFest is a celebration of data in which teams of undergraduates work around the clock to find and share meaning in a large, rich, and complex data set. As a competitor, you will have the chance to win prizes, collaborate with some of the best mathematicians and designers at Brown, and eat delicious free food. That being said, you don't have to compete to be able to participate in our workshops, eat our food, or participate in our networking events with cool data scientists.
