DataFest by American Statistical Association

ASA DataFest is a celebration of data in which teams of undergraduates work around the clock to find and share meaning in a large, rich, and complex data set. As a competitor, you will have the chance to win prizes, collaborate with some of the best mathematicians and designers at Brown, and eat delicious free food. That being said, you don't have to compete to be able to participate in our workshops, eat our food, or participate in our networking events with cool data scientists. Whether intending to compete or to simply participate, fill out the form to apply!

How does it work?

    • We’ll give everyone a *really cool* dataset coming from the American Statistical Association, something that you cannot access on your own
      We’ll accept 30-50 people, with groups
      You’ll take the data, build cool models and perform visualizations, and show it to judges, and potentially win money!
      We’ll have food, drinks, and swag
      Prizes go to best visualization, best presentation/show, best use of external data
      Workshops on cool data tools for people with varied levels of experience (CS/Python stuff)
